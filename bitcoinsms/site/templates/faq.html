Frequently Asked Questions


This service doesn't require an account. It doesn't want your email, or financial details. It won't store cookies or track personal details.
What is Bitcoin?
Bitcoin is the future. It's a technology that allows for the decentralization and freeing of resources that have traditionally been centralized and controlled by small groups of people. You can start your journey on Bitcoin.org.
Why are only numbers in the United States supported?
This service was built as a "weekend project". Only supporting the United States was the easiest way. With enough demand it's possible support for other countries may be added, but it's not promised.
How many Bitcoin confirmations does the service require?
This service accepts and sends your text message with zero confirmations as long as your transaction is accepted by it's full node as valid.
How long does it take to send my message?
Usually once you send the payment from your bitcoin wallet it only takes a few seconds.
Can I prepay for messages to avoid paying a Bitcoin transaction fee for every message?
This service doesn't want to store bitcoin for anyone. If you are using the API you could create multiple messages and pay for them all with a single transaction to save on fees.
What number are my messages sent from?
The service maintains a pool of numbers that sends out the text messages. The from number will not always be the same. It is not possible to choose what number your message will be from.
Can messages be replied to?
Messages cannot be replied to at this time. In the future a feature may be added to receive incoming messages as well.
Is there a limit on volume?
If you are going to send more than 100 messages a day please contact us as a courtesy.
